Sales Dashboard Insights

1.  The South region recorded the highest total sales, while the West
    had the lowest among all regions.
2.  Technology contributed the largest share of category-wise sales,
    slightly higher than Furniture and Office Supplies.
3.  Monthly sales show large fluctuations, with noticeable peaks during
    the early and mid months of the year.
4.  All three product categories performed almost equally, each
    contributing around one-third of total sales.
